Biography

Originally from Belarus, Masha is an award-winning cellist who has played individually, with groups of musicians and multiple orchestras throughout Europe and the United States. A second generation musician; she is currently a principal cellist with the Conservatory at Bard College in Annandale, New York, a short distance from Manhattan and Boston.

Her passionate playing, imbued with the romance of Eastern Europe is intensified by the use of a loop pedal to create a thoroughly modern sound. An experienced performer with an extensive repetoire, Masha mesmerizes with a unique interpretation of pop, jazz and classical works.

Masha Zabara plays an O.Glásel violoncello, 1917, generously provided by Carlsen Cello Foundation.
